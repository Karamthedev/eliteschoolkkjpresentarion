What Are Non-Profits?

Non-profits help others, not for money. They support causes like education, health, and the environment.

Why They Matter

They provide food, education, and support for those in need. They make life better for many people.

Examples

Organizations like the Red Cross and World Wildlife Fund make a big difference globally.

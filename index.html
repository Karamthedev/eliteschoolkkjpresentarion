Welcome!

Welcome! Today we will talk about Non-Profit Organizations. These are special groups that work to help people and make the world a better place.

What Are Non-Profits?

Non-profit organizations don’t work for money. Their goal is to help others by supporting good causes like education, health, and protecting the planet.

Why They Matter

They give food to the hungry, help children learn, care for the sick, and protect nature. They make life better for many people around the world.

Examples

Groups like the Red Cross and the World Wildlife Fund help in many ways — from saving lives to protecting animals and the environment.

Thank You!

Thank you for watching! Remember, even small acts of kindness can make a big change in the world.

What Are Non-Profits?

Non-profits are organizations that work to help others, not for profit. They focus on causes like education, health, and the environment.

Why They Matter

They provide essential services, support communities, and create positive change in society.

Examples

Organizations like the Red Cross and World Wildlife Fund make a significant difference globally.
